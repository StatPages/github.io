Links to other statistics-related web sites...

  • The Thomeer Excel spreadsheet -- a highly developed piece of spreadsheet software that implements the complete formalism of Bert Thomeer, Ben Swanson, Ed Clerke and Jan Buiting for the type curve matching analysis of Mercury Injection Capillary Pressure data obtained upon porous geological materials. The original version was developed by Dr Ed Clerke and Mr. Paul Martin. Continuously developed for over 15 years and used for thousands of geological samples in the oil and gas industry, the latest version now includes a data editing capability with discussion material. For more background, see the reference papers by J. H. M Thomeer; B. Swanson,; E. A. Clerke; and J. J. M. Buiting in the petroleum industry literature.
  • Free neuro imaging software from Dr. Chris Rorden's Neuropsychology Lab at University of South Carolina. -- Twenty one programs devoted to brain imaging and physiology of the brain.
  • HTML for Beginners -- A nice introduction to HTML, describing the concept of markup tags, preparing an HTML document, using meta-tags, basic formatting, hyperlinks and anchors, images, forms and tables.
